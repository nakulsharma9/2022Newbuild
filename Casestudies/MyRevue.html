Overview

MyRevue is a 60-second product discovery and review platform driven by consumers. MyRevue users tell brands about their product and service experiences.

Context

With the advent of e-commerce and the internet sector, an increasing number of people are purchasing goods online. However, with so many various sources of information, consumers wind up spending a significant amount of time and energy just browsing and studying. It has also gotten more difficult to make simple purchasing selections.

Project Goal:

The primary goal of this project was to develop a platform where shoppers could access insightful reviews and genuine use scenarios. With this, we hope to eliminate or lessen indecision, doubt, or the likelihood of choosing inferior items or services.

Research:

To begin with the project goal, we spent some time gathering data from user studies and information obtained from Google Form surveys. Following the completion of the surveys, we did some fundamental analysis to gain a clear understanding of flow.
